Pretty much all of the testing here will be through the console.

Available function signatures to try to mutate state of variables:

  • declare_var(context, var_type, var_name, var_value);
    Three options for context:
    • "globalState"
    • "globalScratch"
    • "local"
  • set_var(var_type, var_name, var_value);
  • get_value_of_var(var_name);
  • enter_scope();
  • exit_scope();
  • push_context();
  • pop_context();
